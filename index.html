About

Hello, my name is Zaynaib Giwa. I believe that community engagement is a door that opens to success. I am currently a co-organizer for the RLadies Chicago and People of Color Code meetup groups where I host hack nights, workshops and speeches for beginner programmers. I also have two blogs about my adventures of learning data science and my medium blog journaling about becoming a developer in Chicago. In my spare time if I am not attending a hackathon or coding with some of my best buds; I am on Instagram stalking foodies or hanging out at a library.
